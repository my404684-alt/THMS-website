Visual Narratives &
Architectural Studies

Before the lens, there was the lead. These graphite and charcoal studies serve as the primary documentation of structural integrity, capturing the "soul" of these monuments before modern intervention.

Every stroke is a measurement; every shadow is a record of light-play against ancient stone. We utilize these tactile renderings to guide our digital twin reconstructions.

Appendix / Graphite Ref.

The sketch is a form of slow-looking. By tracing the columns of Luxor or the symmetry of the Taj, we uncover architectural secrets often missed by rapid laser scanning.
